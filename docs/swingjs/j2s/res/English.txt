GAME HINTS
Dig to the bottom of the sea as fast as you can! \n\nMine items and sell them at the shop so you can afford upgrades to your sub.
LEFT/4: \nDig/move to the left \n\nRIGHT/6: \nDig/move to the right \n\nUP/2: \nSurface \n\nDOWN/8: \nDig down
Holding the UP/2 or DOWN/8 keys for 5 seconds activate auto-pilot. \n\nOnce engaged, you'll keep moving without needing to hold the key. \n\nNOTE: Auto-pilot is disabled if you hit something, go to the cruiser, start drilling or press a different direction key.
The game stores various statistics relating to your gameplay. View these by selecting the 'Statistics' option from the main menu. \n\nNOTE: You can clear your statistics (and saved game data) by selecting the 'Clear Saved Data' option from the OPTIONS menu.
Your score is displayed when you have finished the game or have died. \n\nIt is calculated based on the time it took you to finish or die. Of course, the score will be bigger if you win...
At the end of the game you will be asked you if you want to post your score to the Leaderboard. If you do, it will appear on the game's leaderboards. \n\nIMPORTANT: You may be charged for data traffic when submitting your score.
Select 'Leaderboards' from the main menu or game over screen to view the high scores submitted by other players. \n\nIMPORTANT: You may be charged for data traffic when viewing the leaderboards.
Sell mined items so you can afford sub upgrades. \n\nRarer materials are found the further down you dig. \n\nBuy items at the shop to make your life easier. \n\nIf you hold the up or down keys for a few seconds you'll activate your sub's auto-pilot.
